Reading magmatic systems

I am a petrologist and mineralogist working on mafic-ultramafic systems, platinum-group elements, oxygen isotopes, and critical minerals. My research moves from field geology and petrography to microanalytical work, thermodynamic modelling, and geochemical interpretation.

Research focus

What drives the work

Layered intrusions

Understanding Ni-Cu-PGE systems, chromitites, and the petrogenesis of the Bushveld Critical Zone in the context of the energy transition.

PGE mineralogy

Tracking platinum-group minerals, alloys, oxides, and their overprints from field textures to high-resolution analytical data.

Melt-rock interaction

Using mineral textures, inclusion assemblages, and geochemistry to decode how melts and fluids reshape ultramafic systems.

Early Earth isotopes

Applying triple oxygen and hydrogen isotopes to constrain crustal recycling, hydrothermal alteration, and ancient surface processes.
