Conference history

Talk list

  • IMSG 2026, Barberton, South Africa. Oral presentation: "Mottled anorthosites and discordant ultramafic pipes in the MG4-UG1 interval of the Upper Critical Zone, Bushveld Complex".
  • Geocongress 2025, Bloemfontein, South Africa. Oral presentation: "Petrogenesis of the Akanvaara layered intrusion".
  • MDSG 2024, Edinburgh. Poster: "Assessing mass-balance of layered intrusions: the Akanvaara intrusion case study in Lapland, Finland".
  • Goldschmidt 2023, Lyon, France. Remote oral talk: "Early Earth's hydrosphere: insights from the hydrothermally altered submarine lavas of the Eoarchean Saglek-Hebron Complex, Canada".
  • 14th International Platinum Symposium 2023, Cardiff, UK. In-person oral presentation: "Origin of native osmium in metamorphosed peridotites: insights from multiphase inclusions (Adamsfield placer, Tasmania)".
  • Virtual Early Career Platinum Symposium 2022. Oral presentation: "Noble metals in arc basaltic magmas worldwide: a case study of modern and pre-historic lavas of the Tolbachik volcano, Kamchatka".
  • EGU General Assembly 2021. Oral presentation: "Awaruite (Ni3Fe) as a platinum-group elements concentrator: Preliminary data".
  • SGA 2019 Conference, Glasgow, United Kingdom. In-person oral presentation: "Platinum mineralization in Ural-Alaskan complexes of Koryak Highlands (Far East Russia)".
