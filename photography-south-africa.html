Magnet Heights

Field trip in April 2025. I just arrived in South Africa; our colleagues from Oxford had too. Here we are at Magnet Heights, on the eastern limb of the Bushveld Igneous Complex.

A fieldwork entry that can hold explanation of locality, stratigraphic position, scale, and the practical side of reading an outcrop under real weather conditions. It is also a useful place for adding sampling coordinates and a map link for future teaching material.
