Image folders are organized for reuse across research pages, blog posts, and photography sections.

- `headers/`: page hero images such as `research-bear.jpg` and `cv-bear.jpg`
- `portraits/`: reusable portraits and cutouts of Anton
- `research/`: research covers, figures, and scientific image assets
- `photography/`: field and locality photographs for gallery pages
- `hobbies/`: hobby-related images
- `archive/`: screenshots, sidecar files, and other non-site assets kept for reference

Naming style:
- use lowercase kebab-case
- describe subject first, then place or context when useful
- examples: `anton-field-portrait.jpg`, `kamchatka-tolbachik-volcano.jpg`, `pgm-band-micrograph.webp`
